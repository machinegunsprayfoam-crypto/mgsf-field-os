# Lead Intake Schema

Use this as the standard data model for forms, CRM, spreadsheets, Zapier, Google Drive automations, and future FieldOS app screens.

## Customer

- customer_name
- company_name
- phone
- email
- preferred_contact_method
- billing_address
- project_address

## Project

- service_type
- project_category: residential | commercial | agricultural | industrial | government | subcontractor
- project_description
- square_feet
- dimensions
- thickness_inches
- board_feet
- access_conditions
- substrate_or_surface
- current_problem
- desired_outcome
- urgency
- target_start_date
- budget_range
- decision_maker
- photos_received
- drawings_received

## Service type options

- Spray foam insulation
- Commercial SPF roofing system
- Roof coating
- Concrete lifting and leveling
- Void filling
- Soil stabilization
- Polyurea coating
- Building performance service

## Sales status

- New lead
- Needs info
- Qualified
- Estimate started
- Proposal sent
- Follow-up needed
- Won
- Lost
- Scheduled
- Completed

## Required follow-up if missing

- Photos
- Measurements
- Address
- Access details
- Desired timeline
- Decision-maker confirmation
